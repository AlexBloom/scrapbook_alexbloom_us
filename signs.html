Signs

Signs tell stories about our culture, economy, and values that are not written in history books. The ideals and attitudes of America have been carefully recorded; we can see our nation's character on the front of grocers, garages, bars, and arenas. Within these mementos, where time has been paused, we can observe the aspects of culture that we have gained and lost.

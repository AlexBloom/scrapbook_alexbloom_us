Memory

Anticipating wild weather and tough terrain, I shot most of my portraits from the trip on an old plastic Holga that I had no fear of destroying. Some of the negatives never made it, and most came out blurry, but I think they tell our story quite well. A beautiful thing happens when a photo is just clear enough to help you recall a time and place: may your memory do the rest.
